Polygon.Leaderboards

Description

Polygon-Leaderboards is a web application which extends features provided in Polygon.Scoring. Also, this service gives the opportunity for users to compete in the accuracy and efficiency of their developed models by publishing the results of metrics for a specific task in leaderboards.

User Guide

To use Polygon-Leaderboards applicaton just follow several steps:

  1. Create an account or sign in if you already have one

  1. Go to the "Upload" page
  1. Select the desired data modality
  1. Fill the form with your model and data file, input data size
  1. Submit the form

  1. Publish your results and view them in leaderboard

  1. Manage your published results in "User Page"
